Cancel options

Clarity for your guests. Guaranteed revenue for you

Cancellations are an unavoidable part of hospitality. No matter how well we plan, unexpected events can force guests to change or cancel their bookings. When that happens, it’s important that they know what to expect – just as it’s important for hosts to better plan and avoid losing out on revenue.

That is why we have established very simple rules around cancellation. Hosts can choose between two tariffs:

Fully Refundable:

Hosts can determine up to which point the reservation can be cancelled and fully refunded. Hosts can determine how many days before the planned check-in date guests will be fully refunded. They can also choose to fully refund reservations at any time, including up to the check-in date. We still highly recommend our users to notify the property of their cancellation at the earliest possible date.

Non-Refundable:

These tariffs are not refundable once booked. Such tariffs usually require a partial or full upfront payment.

Information about the applicable tariff can be found in the room selection area when making a reservation. We recommend you read the text there carefully. Usually it states the date until which you can cancel the reservation. In case of a Non-Refundable tariff it will state so.

Cancellations can be done by logging into our platform and selecting the desired reservation you would like to cancel and click on cancel. You will get a confirmation by email at the end. Reservations can also be cancelled by notifying our customer service team via email or phone.

Hosts can adjust their cancellation policies at any point on the property registration and management section of the portal.
